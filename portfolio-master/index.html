About Me

Hi, my name is Conor! I am currently a student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am persuing a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and a minor in Technology, Arts and Media. I am projected to graduate in May of 2017. I was born in Reno, Nevada in May of 1995. I moved to Colorado roughly a year after and have lived here ever since.

Originally I wanted to become a professional musician. I played in a metal band all through high school and eventually found that passion was unrealistic and would not get me as far as I wanted to take it. Eventually I enrolled as a student in Computer Science and fell in love with it. In a professional career, I am looking for something I can be passionate about, and at this point in my life, I feel that advanced Javascript and design work will take me far. My dream job is to work at Google, but who's isn't?
